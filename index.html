Battles Of World War 2

World War 2 lasted a total of 6 long fighting years and had many battles.

The Battle of France.

The Battle of France, also known as the Fall of France, was the German invasion of France and the Low Countries in 1940 during the Second World War.

Learn more

Operation Overlord.

Operation Overlord was the code name for the Battle of Normandy, the Allied operation that launched the successful invasion of German-occupied Western Europe during World War II. The operation was launched on 6 June 1944 with the Normandy landings.

Learn more

Battle of Guadalcanal.

The Guadalcanal Campaign, also known as the Battle of Guadalcanal and code-named Operation Watchtower, originally applying only to an operation to take the island of Tulagi, by Allied forces.

Learn more

Battle of Stalingrad.

The Battle of Stalingrad was a major battle on the Eastern Front of World War II in which Nazi Germany and its allies fought the Soviet Union for control of the city of Stalingrad in Southern Russia, on the eastern boundary of Europe.
